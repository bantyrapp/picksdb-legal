Creator tipping in chats (virtual coins)

Inside any live chat, thread, or group chat, viewers may send the room’s creator a voluntary PicksDb Coins™ tip via the in-app gift flow. Tips are virtual currency, not real money: they are deducted from the sender’s coin balance and credited to the recipient’s coin balance for in-app use. Tips do not generate Stripe payouts, are not wagers or contest entries, and are not purchases of specific services from the creator. Non-anonymous tips post a “[username] tipped X coins” system message into the room and appear on the room’s per-room Top Tippers leaderboard; anonymous tips are credited but the tipper’s identity is redacted in chat and on the leaderboard. Tips are voluntary and final once credited; see the Terms of Service (Section 3b) for details.

Chat moderation (hosts & moderators)

Live chats, threads, and group chats are run by the user who created them (the “host” or “creator”). Hosts may promote up to 10 other users per room to moderator. Moderators may mute or unmute participants, remove participants, delete messages, and approve join requests, but cannot mute, remove, or demote the host or other moderators. Muted participants can still view the room but cannot post until unmuted. Host- and moderator-level actions are user-initiated and are not official PicksDb LLC decisions; PicksDb LLC may still review or override any in-room action. See the Terms of Service (Section 8d) for details.

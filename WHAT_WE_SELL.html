What We Sell

PicksDb is a content creator platform where users can subscribe to content creators to access their UFC and MMA knowledge, insights, and expertise.

For Users

Users can purchase monthly subscriptions to access exclusive content from content creators. Subscribers receive:

  • Access to creator insights and analysis on fighters and events
  • Creator lineups and research-based predictions
  • Exclusive content from creators who have done research on specific fighters and matchups
  • Ability to bookmark favorite fighters and events for entertainment purposes
  • Personalized content feed based on subscribed creators

For Content Creators

Content creators can monetize their expertise by sharing their knowledge with subscribers. Creators can:

  • Create insights and analysis on fighters and events
  • Share lineups based on their research
  • Set their own monthly subscription price
  • Build a subscriber base of users interested in their expertise
  • Share exclusive content with their subscribers

Platform Features

Our platform provides:

  • Subscription-based access to creator content
  • Fighter and event bookmarking for entertainment purposes
  • Content discovery and creator profiles
  • Monthly subscription management
  • Secure payment processing for subscriptions

All content is created by independent content creators who share their research, insights, and analysis with subscribers who pay a monthly fee to access their exclusive content.

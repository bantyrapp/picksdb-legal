What We Sell - PicksDb

Last Updated: January 2025

Our Business Model

PicksDb is a subscription-based content creator platform, similar to Patreon, OnlyFans, or Substack. We enable MMA (Mixed Martial Arts) content creators to monetize their expertise through monthly subscriptions.

What We Sell:

  • Monthly Subscriptions: Users pay a monthly fee to access creator content
  • Creator Content: Predictions, analysis, and insights about MMA events
  • Entertainment Content: All content is for entertainment purposes only
  • Subscription Access: Users subscribe to creators to view their exclusive predictions and analysis

How It Works

  1. Content creators set their own monthly subscription price ($9.99 - $199.99/month)
  2. Users subscribe to creators they want to follow
  3. Subscribers gain access to the creator's predictions, analysis, and insights
  4. Creators receive payments through Stripe Connect Express
  5. Platform fee: 2% on subscriptions (Stripe processes payments and charges 3%, total 5% fees)

What We Do NOT Sell

We explicitly do NOT sell:

  • ❌ Betting services or gambling
  • ❌ Sportsbook services
  • ❌ Prizes or rewards based on prediction outcomes
  • ❌ Odds-making services
  • ❌ Contests or competitions with monetary prizes
  • ❌ Financial returns based on prediction accuracy
  • ❌ Wagering or betting platforms
  • ❌ Any service that pays out winnings

Payment Processing

All payments processed through our platform are for subscription access to creator content only. No payments are tied to:

  • Betting outcomes
  • Event results
  • Prediction accuracy
  • Gambling activities
  • Performance-based rewards

Content Nature

All content on our platform (predictions, picks, analysis) is for entertainment and discussion purposes only. It represents:

  • Non-expert opinions (similar to sports analysis shows)
  • Entertainment content, NOT betting advice
  • NOT financial advice or professional advice
  • NOT gambling recommendations

Terminology

We use prediction-based terminology throughout our platform:

  • "Predictions" (not "bets" or "wagers")
  • "Forecasts" and "Analysis" (not "betting advice")
  • "Confidence" (not "risk" or "stake")
  • "Creator Insights" (not "betting tips")

No gambling terminology appears anywhere in our user interface.

Compliance

We operate as a content creation platform (restricted business category, not prohibited). We:

  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations
  • Have comprehensive Terms of Service that prohibit gambling activities
  • Use Stripe Connect Express for creator payouts
  • Actively moderate content to remove gambling-related material
  • Explicitly prohibit all gambling activities in our Terms of Service

Comparison to Similar Platforms

Our business model is identical to approved content creator platforms:

  • Patreon: Creators monetize content through subscriptions
  • OnlyFans: Content creators charge monthly fees for exclusive content
  • Substack: Writers monetize newsletters through subscriptions
  • YouTube Premium: Users pay for exclusive content access

The only difference is our content focuses on MMA predictions/analysis instead of other types of content. The business model is the same: subscription-based content creator platform.

Summary

We sell monthly subscriptions to access creator content. That's it. We do not sell betting services, gambling, prizes, or any outcome-based rewards. All content is for entertainment purposes only.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 27, 2025

Introduction

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices.

Trust & Safety: Encryption in transit • Access controls • Monitoring • We don't sell personal data • Payments handled securely by Stripe; we don't store full bank details

1. Quick Summary

High-level overview of our privacy practices:

  • We collect account, activity, and limited device data to operate Picksdb.
  • We use service providers (authentication, cloud hosting, analytics, payments) under contracts with data protection safeguards.
  • Public content you post (e.g., posts, comments) is visible by design.
  • We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties.
  • You can manage settings, request data export or deletion, and contact us with privacy requests at support@picksdatabase.com.
  • If you enable creator payouts, onboarding and banking happen via a payment partner; we do not store your full bank details.

2. What We Collect

Complete list of data we process:

  • Account Data: name, username, email address, profile photo, bio, and profile details you provide.
  • Activity Data: posts, comments, likes, follows, shares, predictions, picks, and basic app usage analytics (features used, time spent).
  • Communication Data: chat messages, direct messages, live chat streams, thread posts, room messages, message timestamps, recipients, chat room IDs, message reactions, and all content you send through communication features.
  • Prediction Data: predictions you make, odds information you view, hypothetical tracking data (units, hypothetical winnings calculations) - all for entertainment purposes only.
  • Social Data: users you follow, users who follow you, profile views, social interactions, and social feed activity.
  • Device/Technical Data: device identifiers, IP address, app version, operating system, diagnostic logs (for security and reliability), crash reports.
  • Location Data: approximate location (region/country) for compliance purposes.
  • Preferences: notification settings, topics you follow, fighters you follow, app preferences.
  • Moderation Data: reports you file, content moderation actions, policy violation records, communications flagged for review.
  • Creators (if applicable): payout and tax information collected by our third-party payment partner (we do not store full bank details or government ID numbers).

3. How We Use Data

Why we collect and process your data:

  • Service Delivery: create and secure your account; render feeds, profiles, predictions, odds displays, chat rooms, direct messages, live chat streams, and app features.
  • Communication Features: deliver your messages to recipients, display chat rooms and threads, enable live chat streams, facilitate user-to-user communications.
  • Entertainment Features: display odds information (for informational purposes only), calculate hypothetical tracking data (units, hypothetical winnings) for entertainment purposes only.
  • Personalization: tailor content recommendations and app experience based on your activity and preferences.
  • Safety & Moderation: detect, investigate, and help prevent abuse, spam, fraud, policy violations, and illegal activity; monitor communications for safety; enforce Terms of Service; review reported content and messages.
  • Analytics & Improvements: understand app usage patterns to improve features and fix bugs (aggregated, anonymized data).
  • Legal Compliance: comply with applicable laws, respond to legal requests, enforce our Terms, protect rights and safety, provide communications data to law enforcement when required.
  • Communication: send you service notifications, updates, and responses to your requests.

4. Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

We may share limited data with:

  • Service Providers (authentication providers, cloud hosting, analytics partners, payment processors, communication infrastructure providers) under strict contracts with data protection safeguards and purpose limitations.
  • Law Enforcement when required by law, court order, or to protect safety (including communications data and message content).
  • Business Transfers in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with user notification).
  • Public Content you post is visible by design to other users on the platform.
  • Communications you send are delivered to intended recipients (chat messages, direct messages, live chat streams).
  • Other users in chat rooms, threads, and public communications can see your messages and profile information.

We never share your personal data for marketing purposes without your consent.

Important Note: Communications sent through our platform are NOT private or encrypted. We may access, monitor, and store all communications for safety, moderation, and legal compliance.

5. Payments & Stripe

How payments are processed securely:

Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store full payment card numbers or full bank details on our servers. Stripe acts as a payment processor/PISP and may process personal data under its own terms and privacy policy. We receive limited status information (e.g., subscription status, payout state) to operate features.

6. Community Safety & Moderation

How we process data to keep Picksdb safe:

To enforce policies and keep the platform safe, we collect and process:

  • Content you post (posts, comments, messages, chat messages, direct messages, live chat streams) for moderation purposes.
  • Reports you file about other users or content.
  • Moderation actions taken (warnings, restrictions, bans).
  • Metadata associated with content (timestamps, device info, IP addresses, chat room IDs, message recipients).
  • Automated detection signals (keyword filtering, spam detection, policy violation patterns, inappropriate content detection).

Human moderators review automated flags. We may monitor, review, and store communications (including private messages) for safety, moderation, legal compliance, and to prevent abuse. We log policy actions and preserve relevant data for dispute handling, legal obligations, and safety. We retain moderation data and communications data as long as necessary for safety and legal compliance, typically up to 12 months after account closure. We may share communications data with law enforcement when required by law or to protect safety.

7. Data We Do Not Collect

What we avoid storing:

We do not store full payment card numbers, full bank account details, government ID numbers (except as necessary for creator onboarding via payment partners), or biometric data. If future features require sensitive data, we will provide clear notice and obtain appropriate consent.

8. Age, Location & Compliance

Protecting minors and honoring local laws:

Picksdb is NOT directed to children under 18. We do NOT knowingly collect personal information from users under 18. We require users to be 18+ to create accounts. If we discover that we have collected data from a minor, we will delete it immediately and terminate the account.

We apply age restrictions (18+) and may use coarse location (e.g., region/country) to comply with applicable laws and to disable features that could be construed as wagering. We do not provide a sportsbook or prize contests. We may adjust features by jurisdiction where required.

9. Legal Bases (EEA/UK)

Contract, legitimate interests, legal obligations, consent:

  • Contract: to provide Picksdb and requested features.
  • Legitimate interests: to keep Picksdb secure and improve features, balanced against your rights.
  • Legal obligation: to comply with applicable laws.
  • Consent: where required (e.g., certain notifications or analytics). You may withdraw consent at any time.

10. International Transfers

Cross‑border processing and safeguards:

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. Where applicable, we rely on appropriate safeguards (such as contractual clauses) for cross‑border transfers.

11. Creators & Payments

Third‑party onboarding, payouts, and tax information:

If you enable creator features and payouts, payment onboarding (including bank account and identity verification) is handled by our third‑party payment partner. Picksdb does not store your full bank details. The payment partner processes payout and tax information subject to its own terms and privacy policy. We may receive limited status information to operate creator features.

12. Your Controls & Rights

Manage account data and exercise privacy rights:

You can:

  • Update or delete profile information in Settings
  • Control notification preferences
  • Request a copy of your data (data export)
  • Request account deletion
  • Request correction of inaccurate data

California residents (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal information we collect, delete your personal information, opt-out of sale (we do not sell data), and non-discrimination.

EU/UK residents (GDPR): You have rights to access, rectification, erasure, portability, restriction, and objection.

To exercise any rights, contact support@picksdatabase.com. We will respond within 30 days.

13. Security

Safeguards we apply and your role in security:

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards: encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest where supported; access controls and authentication; monitoring for unauthorized access; regular security assessments.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for: using a strong password; keeping your login credentials confidential; reviewing your connected devices; reporting suspicious activity.

If you believe your account has been compromised, contact support@picksdatabase.com immediately.

14. Data Retention

How long we keep data and why:

We retain personal data as long as necessary for: Service provision (while your account is active); Legitimate business needs (security, fraud prevention); Legal compliance (tax records, legal holds, dispute resolution).

Retention periods:

  • Account data: until account deletion request
  • Content you post: until deletion or account closure
  • Moderation data: up to 12 months after account closure
  • Analytics data: 24 months maximum, then anonymized
  • Legal/transaction records: 7 years for tax and legal compliance

Deleted accounts are permanently removed within 90 days. When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or de-identify it.

15. Children

Age limitations and children's data:

Picksdb is NOT directed to children under 18. We do NOT knowingly collect personal data from users under 18. We require users to be 18+ to create accounts. If we discover that we have collected data from a minor, we will delete it immediately and terminate the account.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 18 has provided us with personal information, contact support@picksdatabase.com immediately and we will delete the information.

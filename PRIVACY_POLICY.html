5a. Creator Shop — listings, orders & affiliate data

When you operate or purchase from a Creator Shop on PicksDb, we process additional data to operate checkout, enforce shop policies, and protect buyers.

Creators (sellers)

  • Listings: product images, titles, prices, badges, descriptions, Amazon URLs, ASINs, and affiliate tags you provide.
  • Policy acceptance: when you accept seller integrity and Amazon affiliate disclosures in Shop settings, we record that acceptance to enforce publishing rules.
  • Amazon affiliate analytics: we log outbound click events (creator, product, timestamp) shown in your shop dashboard. We do not receive Amazon commission reports, sales totals, or payment data from Amazon on your behalf. You are responsible for your Amazon Associates account privacy and compliance.
  • Orders & payouts (in-app): order line items, paid-at timestamps, Stripe payment intent and charge identifiers, shipping addresses, product snapshots at purchase time, discount/deal snapshots, and whether payment was recorded via app confirmation or payment-partner webhook. Retained for buyer history, fulfillment, chargeback response, and compliance (typically up to 7 years for transaction records).
  • Fraud & enforcement: we may review uploaded product photos and listing details to detect misrepresentation or counterfeit goods. If we identify suspected fraud, we may retain related records longer, share limited data with payment partners (Stripe, Apple) for chargeback and fraud investigation, and disclose information to law enforcement or regulators when required by law.

Buyers (fans)

  • In-app checkout: account identifier, shipping address, order details, payment method type metadata (e.g. Apple Pay, card brand/last four — not full card numbers), transaction timestamps, and product snapshots.
  • Checkout recovery: if payment succeeded but order confirmation was interrupted, limited recovery data may be stored temporarily on your device.
  • Amazon links: when you tap an Amazon link, you leave PicksDb. Amazon’s privacy policy governs data collected on Amazon’s site. We log the outbound click event for creator analytics only.

We do not sell shop purchase data to third parties. We share limited transaction metadata with payment partners to process payments and resolve disputes. Creators who misrepresent products or violate affiliate rules may be permanently banned and reported as described in our Terms of Service — Creator Shop sections.

FTC & fan-facing disclosures

Creators must provide clear disclosures to fans when content includes affiliate links (#ad, paid link, commission earned) as required by FTC guidelines and applicable law. 6a. Crisis support, mental health & threatening behavior

PicksDb is not a mental health provider or crisis service. Chats and posts are for entertainment and community only. If you are in crisis or may harm yourself or others, contact emergency or crisis services immediately—do not rely on PicksDb for help.

If you are in the United States, consider contacting:

  • Emergency: Call 911 (or your local emergency number) if you or someone else is in immediate danger.
  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: Call or text 988 (24/7). Chat at 988lifeline.org.
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741.
  • Veterans Crisis Line: Call 988, then press 1, or text 838255.
  • Other countries: See findahelpline.com or local emergency services.

How PicksDb handles concerning content. If we become aware of content suggesting self-harm, suicide, threats of violence, severe harassment, or exploitation—through user reports, automated signals, or other means—we may remove content, restrict accounts, preserve records, and contact or cooperate with law enforcement or crisis responders where permitted by law and where we believe it is necessary to protect safety. We do not guarantee that we will identify every harmful message or respond in real time.

Reporting in the app. Use in-app Report and Block on posts, comments, messages, and profiles. For urgent safety issues you may also email support@picksdb.com with “Urgent safety” in the subject line. We are not a substitute for 911 or 988.

Depression, anxiety, and emotional distress. Peer chat on PicksDb is not therapy. If you are struggling with mental health, please reach out to a qualified professional or one of the crisis resources above. We may remove content that promotes self-harm or encourages others to harm themselves.
